My Hometown

Banjarbaru is a city in the province of South Kalimantan and its my hometown. Banjarbaru is very green and fresh. You could find many trees around your home if you lived there. In Banjarbaru there are not too many people like in Jakarta, Surabaya or other big cities in Indonesia. About the weather in Banjarbaru, because its location is near with passed by equator line and many forest are there, the weather is not really affected by moonson wind.
